Our Story

25+ Years of Manufacturing Excellence

From a small workshop in Mumbai to a global machinery supplier, our journey has been driven by innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction.

Founded in 1995 by Mr. Rajesh Verma, ABC Manufacturing Machinery started with just 5 employees and a vision to provide high-quality industrial machinery to Indian manufacturers. Today, we have grown into a multinational organization with 200+ employees, serving over 5000 clients across 50+ countries.

Our commitment to quality and continuous innovation has made us a trusted partner for industries ranging from automotive and aerospace to pharmaceuticals and packaging.

Our Journey

Milestones & Achievements

Key moments that shaped our company's growth

1995

Company Founded

ABC Manufacturing Machinery established in Mumbai with 5 employees, starting with basic lathe machines.

2005

ISO 9001 Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2005 certification, establishing robust quality management systems.

2010

International Expansion

Began exporting to Middle East and Southeast Asia. First overseas office opened in Dubai.

2015

New Manufacturing Facility

State-of-the-art manufacturing plant inaugurated in Pune with 50,000 sq. ft. production area.

2018

Product Range Expansion

Launched advanced CNC machines and automation solutions. Crossed 3000 client milestone.

2021

Digital Transformation

Launched e-commerce platform and virtual factory tours. Reached 4500+ clients.

2024

Present Day

Serving 5000+ clients across 50+ countries with 200+ employees and 50+ machine models.
