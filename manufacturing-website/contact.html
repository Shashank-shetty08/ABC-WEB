FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How quickly do you respond to inquiries?

We respond to all inquiries within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please call our sales or support hotlines directly for immediate assistance.

Do you provide international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Our logistics team handles all aspects of shipping, including export documentation, customs clearance, and door-to-door delivery. Shipping costs and timelines vary by destination.

Can I visit your facility to see machines in operation?

Absolutely! We welcome visitors at our Mumbai headquarters and other branch offices. Please schedule an appointment with our sales team in advance so we can arrange a comprehensive demonstration and factory tour.

What information should I provide for an accurate quote?

For the most accurate quote, please provide: machine model or specifications, quantity needed, delivery location (for shipping calculation), any special requirements or customizations, and your timeline. You can attach technical drawings or RFQ documents to your inquiry.

What is your warranty policy?

All ABC Machinery products come with a standard 2-year warranty covering manufacturing defects and parts. Extended warranty options are available for purchase. Our warranty includes technical support and spare parts replacement as per terms and conditions.

Do you offer training for operators?

Yes, we provide comprehensive training programs at our facility or on-site at your location. Training covers basic operation, advanced programming, maintenance procedures, and safety protocols. Customized training packages are available based on your requirements.
